Our Vision

Inspiring women to discover the power of being one in Christ.

We believe every woman is made in God's image and called into a single body — some as warriors, some as peacemakers, all united in spirit and in peace. Be1 exists to break down the walls that divide us, and to unite women to do kingdom work that impacts the world.

Join us as we allow God to unite us as One — one in spirit, one in heart, and one in body.
