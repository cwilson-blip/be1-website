The Role

Lead your group, one in Christ.

Ambassadors recruit a group of 10 or more women to register for the 2027 Be1 Experience, then serve as their point person from sign-up through send-off.

  • — Recruit a group of 10 or more women to register for the 2027 Be1 Experience
  • — Assist group members with the online registration process
  • — Help organize and secure hotel accommodations
  • — Organize any necessary travel arrangements for the group
  • — Pick up and distribute group registration materials at the conference
  • — Serve as a liaison between your group and the Be1 Leadership team
