Who We Are

Our Story & Vision

Be1 exists to inspire women to discover the power of being one in Christ — through experiences that encourage unity and relationship-building within the Christian community.

The Vision

Woven together as one body.

Every woman is made in God's image, and each of us is called into that image differently — some as warriors, some as peacemakers. Be1 exists to encourage women to break down the walls that divide us, and to unite in doing kingdom work that impacts the world.

The mission is simple and wide-reaching: give women everywhere the opportunity to connect, to build networks of mutual support, and to understand our collective strength as the body of Christ. That happens through powerful speakers, worship experiences, honest reflection, and a marketplace of women-owned vendors.

Beyond the Conference

A Resource for the Whole Year

Be1 has hosted virtual conversations addressing pandemic recovery and race relations, with plans to continue fostering understanding across differences. We connect women through ministries and collaborative projects with global Christian impact.
